I developed an SDOH campaign intake and reporting framework that ingested external referral-feed data, standardized social-determinant referral categories, linked referral and fulfillment events at the member level, and produced reporting views for outreach performance monitoring. The goal was to convert raw SDOH referral transactions into analytically usable member-level and referral-type-level datasets, measure whether referrals were fulfilled and how quickly fulfillment occurred, and organize those results into monthly, overall, and post-COVID cohort reporting structures that could support campaign evaluation for the client.
